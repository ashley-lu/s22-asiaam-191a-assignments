Hm. Trying to come up with more things about me is hard. But I will say that I have a crippling coffee addiction (just kidding, I only drink like one cup of caffeinated a day) and I am getting into making my own coffee!

If you're living in the dorms right now I would highly recommend getting an Aeropress. It barely takes up any space if you're concerned about how much space an espresso machine takes up. Also it tastes better than french press and Keurig because it's less grainy and you can control what grounds you're using, respectively.
